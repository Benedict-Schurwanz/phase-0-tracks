Benedict's Marvelous Cat Page

The domestic cat, Latin name "felis catus" is a popular pet in many homes and also a popular resident on many internet sites around the world.

Worth noting here: Cats are (typically) very furry and/or fuzzy creatures.

There are different types of cats in the wild in many places around the world, including (but not limited to)

  • the jungle cat of southeast Asia
  • the European wildcat
  • the African wildcat
  • the Chinese mountain cat
  • and the Arabian sand cat

The scientific classification for the cat species is as follows

  1. Kingdom: Animalia
  2. Phylum: Chordata
  3. Class: Mammalia
  4. Order: Carnivora (see this part means they eat meat)

and then there are several other parts after this one
